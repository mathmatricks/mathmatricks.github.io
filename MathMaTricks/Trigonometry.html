     Pythagoras Theorem (Basic trigonometry)

In this video we discussed about the Pythagoras Theorem also known as Pythagorean Theorem. This theorem gives the relation among the three sides of a Right angled Triangle.It states that in any right triangle square of hypotenuse is the sum of the squares of the other two sides. Pythagoras Theorem is used to find the missing side of a right triangle. We also discussed about the pythagorean triangle and pythagorean triples. In any triangle lengths of all three sides are integers it is known as pythagorean triangle and the sides are pythagorean triples(pythagorean triplet).
